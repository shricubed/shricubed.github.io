What is a CTF?

A Capture The Flag (CTF) competition is a cybersecurity competition where teams solve cyber-related challenges in d different categories (Crypto, Forensics, Web Exploits, etc.)

Resources

CTFTime - calendar of CTFs and compilation of most old CTF problems and writeups (solution guides).

CTF Learn - CTF practice problems of different difficulty levels.

CTF101 - Guide to solving problems in different CTF categories.
